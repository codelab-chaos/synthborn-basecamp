## Client Side
This is a little more involved, but this should be intuitive

1. Navigate to your equivalent of `C:\Users\(Your User)\AppData\Roaming\Hytale\UserData`.
2. Navigate into `Saves`, and enter the save with the universe you want to put the world into.
3. In a new Window/Tab navigate to the packages `worlds` folder.
5. Choose the world(s) you want to go with (if there are multiple), copy the world(s).
6. Go to the Games `Saves` folder, than into the `universe` folder, and paste the world(s).
7. Next time you launch the world, it will now have the new world(s) in the universe.

___

## Server Side
This is a little more involved, but this should be intuitive

1. First Navigate to your equivalent of `D:\Hytale Server\2026.02.19-1a311a592\Server`.
2. In a new Window/Tab navigate to the packages `worlds` folder.
5. Choose the world(s) you want to go with (if there are multiple), copy the world(s).
5. Go to the servers root, than into the `universe` folder, and paste the world(s).
6. When you launch the server, it will now have the new world(s) in the universe.

## How to Navigate to the worlds (Bonus)
Now you may be wondering, how do I navigate into the world? Well its rather simple, all you need to do more or less is `/tp world [World Name]`, the world name should be the same as the folder name, but just in case check with `/world list`.
