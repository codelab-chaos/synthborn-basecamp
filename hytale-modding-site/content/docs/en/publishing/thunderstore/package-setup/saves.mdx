This will be a step by step guide on how to insert your saves into the thunderstore package.

1. First off navigate to your package, and create a folder called `saves`
2. In a new window/tab navigate to your equivalent of `C:\Users\(Your Username)\AppData\Roaming\Hytale\UserData\Saves`.
3. Take the Save that you're after, and place it into the `saves` folder we created.
4. Optional Extra Step, Remove the `.log` and `.bak` files from the root of the saves you carried into the package (modify the ones in the package), and the `Logs` and `backup` folders.
5. And thats all you really need to do.
