This will be a step by step guide on how to insert your worlds into the thunderstore package.

1. First off navigate to your package, and create a folder called `worlds`
2. In a new window/tab navigate to your equivalent of `C:\Users\(Your Username)\AppData\Roaming\Hytale\UserData\Saves`.
3. Navigate into the Save that the universe resides within.
4. Navigate into the `universe` folder.
5. Navigate into the `worlds` folder.
6. Take the world in which you're after, and call it whatever, and than place it into the `worlds` folder we created.
7. And thats all you really need to do.
